What is Shopzilla?

Shopzilla is a well-known e-commerce website and a fashion hub. It has been referred to as "one of the most influential economic and cultural forces in the world" and is one of the world's most valuable brands.

How do I create an account on Shopzilla

You can create your account on Shopzilla by entering and verifying your mobile number. Click on "Create New Account / Sign Up Now" after that and fill up the form to create your Shopzilla account.

How are orders placed on Shopzilla delivered to me?

All orders placed on Shopzilla are dispatched through our own courier service - Shopzilla Logistics or through other courier partners such as Blue Dart, Delhivery Etc.

How can I pay for my order at Shopzilla?

We support the following payment options at Shopzilla: Cash On Delivery (available in selected pin codes), Credit Card, Debit Card, Net banking, Gift Card, Wallet.

What is Shopzilla's Cancellation Policy?

You can now cancel an order when it is in packed/shipped status, as long as the cancel option is available on App/Website/M-site. This includes items purchased on sale also. Any amount paid will be credited into the same payment mode using which the payment was made.

Does Shopzilla deliver products outside India?

No. At this point, Shopzilla delivers products only within India.
